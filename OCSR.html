Opalescence Content Strategy Redesign

Tools Used:

  • Google Trends
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Miro
  • Figma

Project Description:

The scope of this project revolved around redesigning the content of the Opalescence website to best appeal to user needs and to catch up to their competitors. My partner for this project and I were tasked to redesign three pages: the homepage, the product page for their toothpaste, and their find-a-dentist page. Throughout this project we were assigned to design the content around our target audience: people who eat organic protein bars.

  • Strategic Intent Matrix: We created a table designed around the intentions of each page we were working on. This marked the intent of the page, its goals and objectives, its audience, what content was required to meet these goals, how the content should be prioritized, and where that content would live on the site.
  • Content Matrix: We created a content matrix based on awareness, consideration, and satisfaction, the issues based around these factors, and our proposed solutions based on the results of our strategic intent matrix and centering around our personas.
  • User flow diagram: We worked together to create user flow diagrams of the homepage content, FAQ content, and social media content in order to track where certain information would be placed in the site.
  • Wireframes: Based on our research, we created wireframes to make high-priority information easy to access and make the site more appealing to a younger audience than it was previously.
