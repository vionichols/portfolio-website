Intercultural Communication Report

Tools Used:

  • Microsoft Word
  • CultureGrams
  • Hofstede's Country Comparison

Project Description:

I conducted a report on McDonalds’ international presence on the internet and how it changes based on region to represent specific cultures. For this project, I examined the McDonalds Japan website and compared it to its U.S. counterpart using Hofstede’s six dimensions and CultureGrams to relate how Japanese culture is reflected in the McDonalds’ website.
